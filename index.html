We are delighted to announce our distinguished speakers for AI in Cancer Care — bringing together expertise across oncology, clinical research, and artificial intelligence.

Prof. Daniel Truhn

Senior Physician, Laboratory for Artificial Intelligence, Diagnostic and Interventional Radiology, RWTH Aachen, Germany

Talk
Agentic AI systems for oncology workflows
Biography

Daniel is a Professor of Medicine at University Hospital Aachen, where he leads the Laboratory for Artificial Intelligence in Medicine. His work focuses on translating AI methods into clinical routine, with a particular emphasis on accelerating the deployment of AI models in oncology. With a background in both physics and medicine, his research bridges methodological innovation and real-world clinical application. In his talk, he will discuss agentic AI systems and their potential to transform diagnostic and therapeutic decision-making in clinical medicine.
