How Will AI Transform
Cancer Care by 2030?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is rapidly transforming both our daily lives and professional practices and is expected to profoundly reshape how cancer care is delivered in the coming years.

This half-day symposium will bring together clinicians, researchers, and technology experts to explore how AI is being integrated into oncology today ‚Äî and what the next decade may hold. Through practical insights and interdisciplinary discussion, we aim to examine both the opportunities and the open questions surrounding AI in cancer care.

Join us as we reflect on how innovation and clinical expertise can shape the future of patient care.
