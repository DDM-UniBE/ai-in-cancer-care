Highlights & Key Moments

The AI in Cancer Care Symposium 2026 is packed with keynote talks, expert panels, and real-world insights shaping the future of AI in oncology.

Spotlight
Opening Keynote

A visionary opening session exploring how agentic AI systems are transforming oncology, setting the stage for the future of autonomous AI in cancer diagnosis and treatment.

Spotlight
Patient Perspectives: Reality Check

A grounding and powerful session sharing the views and priorities of patients and their next of kin, exploring what AI-driven care truly means for those living with cancer.

All Afternoon
Trade Display

Running throughout the entire afternoon, the Trade Display showcases live demos and exhibits from the leading organisations driving AI forward in oncology. Come explore, ask questions, and discover what's next.

Spotlight
Ethics & AI Governance

A critical examination of bias, accountability and the responsible deployment of AI in clinical oncology, designing cancer AI solutions with robustness in mind.

Social
Networking Apéro

Close the day by connecting with researchers, clinicians and industry leaders over drinks. An informal setting to spark new collaborations and conversations.
