Join an interdisciplinary community at the University of Bern to discuss responsible, deployable AI in oncology — with a practical focus on implementation, validation, and clinical impact.

About the event

AI in Cancer Care connects medical imaging, clinical decision support, and real-world deployment — with speakers spanning clinical practice and machine learning research.

Expect concise talks, a moderated discussion, and time to connect with peers. This is designed as a half-day format — sharp, focused, and actionable.

Interested in supporting? Sponsors and partners help us keep the event accessible and impactful.

A short, high-impact program — designed to bring clarity, practical insights, and the right conversations.
